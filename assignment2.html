Websites about Protected Areas Issues

Many species and the health of our ecosystems rely on protected areas for survival. These areas provide secure environments where wildlife can live and thrive, shielded from the impacts of human activity and population growth. Deforestation, pollution, and climate change are rapidly altering natural ecosystems, but protected areas help buffer these environments from such threats. They also store carbon, aiding in the fight against climate change, while playing a crucial role in preserving water sources and biodiversity. The links below highlight the importance of protected areas and the various methods being used worldwide to preserve them for future generations.
